OUR MISSION

Our mission at the Games Club is to provide a welcoming and inclusive environment for individuals of all different backgrounds, experiences and skill levels to come together and enjoy a variety of games. From chess to monopoly and scrabble to many more, we strive to offer a diverse selection that caters to all interests.


We believe that playing games is not only a fun pastime, but also a valuable way to build social connections, improve critical thinking and problem-solving skills, and also reduce stress. By promoting a culture of sportsmanship and fair play, to create an atmosphere that generates competitive and casual play alike. At the Games Club, we are dedicated to creating a fun, engaging, and supportive community where people can come together to enjoy games and build lastingÂ friendships.


OUR VISION

Our vision is to become a leading club in the institution that provides a diverse range of games and activities for students. We aim to create a welcoming and inclusive environment where students can make new friends and develop their skills. We aim to be a club which can go out for competitions ,win and represent the institution in a reputable manner. We want to organize events that bring together students from different backgrounds and interests. Our ultimate goal is to foster a sense of community and belonging among our members.
